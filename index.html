Why This Problem Can't Wait

Aging Workforce Crisis

The average Japanese hunter is 70 years old. Manual mountain patrols—often solo, at night—are physically unsustainable and increasingly dangerous for this critical workforce.

Core Functions

What the System Does

Onboard Thermal Detection

Real-time identification of boars and deer under dense forest canopies using Zenmuse H30T thermal imagery processed entirely on Manifold 3—no cloud round-trip.

Route Optimization

AI pattern recognition maps wildlife movement trails from repeated detections, suggesting statistically optimal trap placement coordinates to ground teams.

Automated Alerting

Seamless integration with ground IoT traps. Hunters receive push notifications only when capture probability exceeds threshold—eliminating empty-trap checks entirely.

Innovation Points

What Sets AX Wildlife Apart

100% inference runs natively on Manifold 3 (100 TOPS). Viable for the off-grid reality of Japanese mountains with zero cellular dependency during patrol.

System is designed to multiply hunter effectiveness—boosting "catch per hour"—rather than automating them out. Hunters remain essential, just empowered.

Core AI inference stack is optimized for DJI Enterprise hardware but exposes open APIs for integration with third-party ground IoT sensors and trap systems.
